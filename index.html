// how to play
01
Drag to Aim
Click and drag anywhere on the screen to set your shot direction and power. The further you drag, the harder the shot. Release to fire. The aim line shows exactly where the ball will go.
02
Navigate Terrain
Different surfaces change how your ball moves. Fairway is fast. Rough slows you down. Sand nearly stops you. Water resets your ball and adds a penalty stroke. The green is slow and precise — putt carefully.
03
Sink the Putt
Get the gold Ð coin into the flagged cup in as few strokes as possible. Each hole has a par score to beat. Rotate the isometric view with R to find the best angle. Under par earns a birdie or eagle.

// level editor
Build Your Own Hole
The built-in editor lets you paint tiles, place obstacles, set the tee and cup, and configure hazards — all in the browser. No external tools. No coding. Click to paint, drag to fill, press E to toggle. Your hole is live the moment you hit Play.
🔗
// mint link
Share Any Hole Instantly
Every hole you build gets a permanent shareable URL. Post it anywhere — Twitter, Discord, Reddit, a text message. Anyone who clicks it plays your hole immediately. No account required for either creator or player. Play counts are tracked.
🌀
// iso rotation
4-Angle Isometric View
Press R to rotate the isometric camera through four angles — NW, NE, SE, SW. A shot that looks blocked from one angle might open up from another. The physics never change — only your perspective does.

// 9 holes
A Full Course, Handcrafted
Nine unique holes with different layouts, terrain mixes, and obstacles. Straight corridors, L-shaped dogleg rights, island greens, pipe teleports, moving Shibe blockers, spinning windmills, sand bunkers, and water hazards. Par 29 total.

// air hockey physics
Puck Feel, Golf Rules
The Ð coin ball uses air hockey physics — low friction, high restitution, slides true. It bounces cleanly off walls, ricochets off obstacles, and behaves like a puck on a frictionless surface. Familiar but fresh. The fairway is fast. The rough bites.

// terrain guide
Tee / Fairway
Fast surface. Ball carries full speed. Your standard playing ground.
Rough
Slow surface. Significant friction. Avoid when possible.
Sand Bunker
Near-stop surface. Ball buries in sand. Requires a hard shot out.
Water Hazard
Ball resets to last dry position. Penalty stroke added. Cross via bridge.
Green
Precision surface. Ball slows significantly. Putt carefully.
Bridge
Crosses over water safely. Fast surface. The only safe path on water holes.

// the mission

The internet built a meme
into a movement.
Now we build something
you can walk into.

FunDoge started as a browser game. The game has a level editor. The level editor produces holes. The best holes from the community will be physically constructed at a real-world mini-golf destination — funded by the same community that made a meme coin the most recognizable currency on the internet.

Not a pitch deck. Not a whitepaper. A game first. Then a building.

Phase A
Proof of Demand
FunDoge is live. Players build holes, share links, play the community's best. The game proves the concept works.
Phase B
Land + Legal
Form a legal entity. Secure land — community votes on location. Everything documented and published publicly.
Phase C
First Build
Boutique destination — physical course. Top community-designed holes get built for real. Early contributors get their wallet engraved permanently.
Phase D
Expansion
Full Fun Center. Restaurant. Live events. The joke becomes a landmark people fly in for. The game never stops growing the course.
// why this works
Most projects demand belief before evidence. FunDoge does the opposite — the game earns belief gradually. Play it first. Share a hole. Then decide if the destination is worth supporting. You don't have to believe in the building before you enjoy the game.
